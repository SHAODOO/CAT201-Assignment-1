Project Title: Expense Manager
Description: Expense Manager is a mobile application that serves to
assist users in managing their personal finances in order to be
financially secure. Users will be able to keep track of all their
income and expenses regardless of time and place. It helps users
to manage their expenses in an easier and more organized way
thus providing them a clearer picture of their spending habits and
financial status.
